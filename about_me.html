About Me

Hi! I’m Pedro, a NYC based artist working in theatre and film. My love for performing began at Guaynabo, Puerto Rico, where I am originally from, and I am now a senior at the Fordham University Theatre program.

Acting allowed me to meet great people, find a creative outlet, and have a new way of telling stories (I’m always finding excuses to tell a good story).

At Fordham, I got opportunities to write and direct which I quickly became passionate about. My work primarily focuses on the supernatural aspects of our relationships and the struggle to hold on to love and empathy. With the pandemic doing away with live theatre for over a year, I found film satisfying my need to create. I quickly became in interested in everything from cinematography to editing.

Now, I’m a cofounder of Hypnagogia Entertainment, a film / theatre company focused on creating work that heightens reality to create an understanding of how we build barriers to protect ourselves. You can learn more about Hypnagogia by clicking the button!
